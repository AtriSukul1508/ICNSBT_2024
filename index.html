ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The Computer Society of India (CSI), Kolkata Chapter in association with Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College, Medinipur, West Bengal, India organizing second International Conference on Cryptography, Network Security and Blockchain technology. This International Conference ICNSBT 2023 basically focused on Network Security, Privacy and Blockchain Technology and its Applications. The Conference will be held during 6-8th March 2024 at the Jalpaiguri government engineering college, West Bengal, India. This international Conference will focus on Privacy, Authentication, Digital Watermarking, Cyber Security, Access Control, Security Modelling, Security in Social Networks, Digital Rights Management, Blockchain in the Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain in Cyber Physical Systems, Blockchain in Social Networking, Blockchain in Supply Chain Management, Cryptocurrency. The main objectives are:

  • To meet domain experts from academia, industry and R&D organizations to discuss research concepts
  • To provide an opportunity for scholars to meet domain experts to discuss research concepts, methodologies and career issues in a comfortable and supportive environment
  • To provide the participants constructive feedback and suggestions on their current research and future research directions from peers and experienced researchers
  • To provide a platform for participants to make informal networks with their peers and future colleagues to develop collaborative research
  • To find out the societal benefits or industrial outcomes of the research work towards human welfare and the possible application fields of other inter-disciplinary areas



ABOUT COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA

Computer Society of India (CSI) is the oldest and largest organisation of IT and allied professionals in India. CSI is a non-profit professional society formed dedicated to Computing to exchange views and information, learn and share ideas.

About Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College

Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College (JGEC) is a NAAC-accredited premier institute for quality technical education in India. Established in 1961, the college has produced many talented engineers till now. It is a fully owned autonomous college of the Government of West Bengal affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology. At present, the institute offers six UG courses (CE, CSE, ECE, EE, IT, and ME) and two PG courses (EE and ME) in Engineering and Technology. Regular and qualified teachers contribute to teaching and learning. The aim of the institute is to be an example in teaching, research, and development of personality to create a competent and responsive human resource for society, the country, and the world. The Institute also comes under Sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC The Institute is governed by a Board of Governors (BOG) constituted as per the provisions of the All-India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the affiliating University/State Government norms. The Governing Body consists of eminent educationists and industrialists as members. Support provided by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) under the Higher education department of the Government of West Bengal, efficient leadership, and contribution made by the faculty members, staff, stakeholders, and proactive students contributes to the development of core values of the Institution.

Call For Paper



Author's Guidelines:

Each paper must be original and unpublished work, not submitted for publication elsewhere (copyright infringement issues will be the responsibility of the authors). Papers must be written in good English. The submissions must be anonymous, i.e. without author name(s), affiliation(s), acknowledgements, or obvious references.

  • It should begin with a title, a short abstract, and a list of key words and its introduction should summarize the contributions of the paper. The similarity index for a submitted paper should be below 15%.

  • Papers should be at most 12 pages in total, including references and appendices, using 11 point font and reasonable margins. Extra charges of Rs.300/- or USD 5 per page is required after 10 pages and maximum page limit can be 12.

  • The authors are encouraged to prepare their paper as per the given guidelines.

  • Submitted papers must be in PDF format and should be submitted electronically via easy chair. One full paper registration will cover at most two accepted papers.


Review Process of International Conference on Network Security and Blockchain Technology (ICNSBT 2024):
  • There is call for paper with firm deadline in terms of submission, acceptance, review of revised submission and camera-ready submission. The Editorial board has decided to implement desk review and rejection by the editorial board. On scrutinizing the papers by the editorial board if the papers rejected in the process of desk review that will not assign any review further. All submissions will be screened for plagiarism by iThenticate®, and when identified, the submissions by the same author will be rejected at any stage of the conference even after the conference and after the publication of the proceedings online from the springer. The question of reimbursement of registration fees will not be applicable here.

  • Names and Affiliations of the authors must NOT be included anywhere in the paper during the initial submission. For the peer review purpose, self-references are NOT permitted. If the author needs to refer his/her own paper the reference MUST be made in third person in text description of the submitted article. Any attempt to reveal author details and affiliation at the time of initial submission will lead to rejection summarily of the paper without further processing. The review will be fully Double Blind in nature.

  • All papers that conform to submission guidelines will be evaluated based on originality, technical and/or research content/depth, correctness, relevance to conference, contributions, and readability. Acceptance of papers will be communicated to authors by submission portal. The authors of the accepted papers will be allowed to make corrections in accordance with the suggestions of the reviewers and submit final camera-ready papers within the stipulated deadline. All camera ready papers must be prepared in conformance with reviewer’s report. The Editorial Board isresponsible for correctness of the manuscript including formal presentation and completeness. Editorial Board is also responsible for ensuring that the content of each contribution meet the designated requirements of the publisher. Editorial Board or the Publisher’s decision would be final in this regard. Acceptance based on the recommendation of reviewers will be at sole discretion of the editors.

How to reach the venue:
1.By Air:
The nearest airport to JGEC is Bagdogra Airport, located approximately 55 kilometers away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a pre-booked cab to reach JGEC. The journey by road takes around 1 to 2 hours, depending on the traffic conditions.
2. By Train:
Jalpaiguri Road Station is the nearest railway station to JGEC, situated about 2 kilometers away. From the railway station, you can hire an auto-rickshaw or a taxi to reach JGEC, which should take around 5-10 minutes.
3. By Road:
JGEC is located in Jalpaiguri district, West Bengal, India. If you prefer traveling by road, you can reach Jalpaiguri by bus or private vehicle. There are regular bus services connecting Jalpaiguri to major cities and towns in the region. Once you reach Jalpaiguri, you can navigate towards the JGEC campus using GPS or by asking for directions from locals.
